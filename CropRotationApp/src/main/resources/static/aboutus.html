Optimized Crop Rotation

AI-Driven Crop Rotation for Sustainable Agriculture

About Us

We are a team of final-year B.Tech students from QIS College of Engineering, working together to innovate and contribute to sustainable agriculture through technology.

Our current project focuses on leveraging Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) to optimize crop rotation strategies. By analyzing historical climate data, soil characteristics, and crop yield information, we aim to create an AI-driven solution that enhances agricultural sustainability, improves crop yields, and promotes resource efficiency. Our work represents our dedication to applying cutting-edge technology to solve real-world challenges.
